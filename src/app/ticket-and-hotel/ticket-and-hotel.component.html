Hotel description

Sitting in the peaceful suburb of Liefering this hotel is close to the motorway, the centre of Salzburg and just 5km from the airport. Its location makes it a great choice for both business travellers and tourists who are looking to escape from the noise and commotion of the city. They will be a short drive away from the beautiful Old Town, where they stroll between the Salzburg Cathedral, Mirabellgarten, Hellbrunn Palace, or visit Mozart''s birthplace on Getreidegasse and the Festung Hohensalzburg castle. After a long day of sightseeing or exhausting meetings the guests can relax with a glass of wine or a refreshing cocktail from the onsite, before heading to their comfortable and cosy rooms.
